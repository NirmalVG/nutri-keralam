# **App Name**: NutriKeralam

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Personal Diet Coach: Receive personalized meal plans, nutritional advice, and answer health queries from an AI coach trained on Indian nutrition in 9 Indian languages. The AI tool is powered by LLaMA 3.2 1B.
- Comprehensive Indian Food Database: Access a database of 10,000+ Indian dishes with accurate nutrition data. Log meals, create custom recipes, and browse for specific regional dishes.
- Visual Food Recognition & Logging: Effortlessly log meals by scanning them with your phone's camera, with nutritional data instantly provided through AI food recognition.
- Condition-Specific Diet Plans: Get tailored diet plans for common health conditions like diabetes, PCOS, and pregnancy, customized for Indian cuisine and ICMR guidelines.
- Automated Lab Report Analysis: Upload PDF lab reports (Thyrocare, Dr. Lal PathLabs) to automatically parse key markers and adjust your diet plan for enhanced personalization.
- User Health & Progress Tracking: Track your weight, activity levels, and dietary intake trends over time with visual graphs and summaries.
- Secure User Authentication: Manage user accounts, secure profiles, and personal health data, leveraging PostgreSQL for robust data storage.

## Style Guidelines:

- The app features a light color scheme designed for clarity and a fresh, premium feel. The primary action color is a rich, earthy goldenrod (#BA8F2E), evoking the vibrancy and natural wealth of Kerala and traditional Indian spices. The background is a very light cream (#FBF9EE), promoting readability and a clean aesthetic. A vibrant coral accent (#F46D58) provides high contrast for critical calls to action and important notifications.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (proportional sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward, and crisp title display. Body and UI text font: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif) for neutral, highly readable content, ensuring clarity for diverse user demographics.
- Utilize clean, modern, and vector-based icons. Focus on clarity and ease of understanding, potentially integrating subtle, culturally relevant motifs where appropriate without compromising simplicity. Adhere to components like those found in shadcn/ui.
- Implement a mobile-first, card-based layout for intuitive navigation and organized presentation of complex nutritional data. Prioritize generous use of whitespace to enhance readability and reduce cognitive load, catering to users of varying tech comfort levels.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations for transitions and feedback elements, conveying a premium and responsive user experience. Focus on functionality and user guidance rather than purely decorative effects, consistent with a performant Next.js application.
